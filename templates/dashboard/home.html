Set up an Academy

LMS is a central place to access all of their training materials. With learning paths, they can complete their training in the order you set for them. From the personal dashboard, they can track their progress through courses and see upcoming exams. Put your team in the driver's seat.

Train your employees in their own language

Offering training courses in their own language to optimize their learning experience. The admin dashboard is also available in many languages.

Manage LMS User Roles in Real Time!

With the Drupal User Management Settings you can control the types of activities users are allowed to perform. You can easily assign and modify user roles to grant independency to user groups working within one Learning Management System, manage course registration and customize user profiles!

Create Online Courses in Minutes

An embedded course builder guides you from one step to another with no need to enter the Admin panel, while a set of predefined eLearning patterns spares you adjusting the modules.
